Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and explain the architecture, structure and functionality of basic components of computer system
  • Demonstrate a critical understanding of core data structures and programming concepts, including algorithm computability
  • Critically evaluate the functionality of different types of software, i.e., operating system, utility programs, languages and applications
  • Critically appraise the emerging trends in the field, such as cloud computing, big data, cyber security, and the professional and ethical requirements for dealing with such contemporary computer-based technologies.

    • Computing as a Profession – Responsibilities and Challenges

    The module commenced with a discussion of our responsibilities as computing professionals,the importance of handling data in accordance with applicable data policies and laws in our country of operation.

    The class started a discussion on the European Union General Data Protection Rule (EU GDPR) this week and I wrote about the data policy in my workplace and how it is influenced by the GDPR. In particular, it was interesting to learn that organisations operating outside the EU have to comply with the GDPR as long as they are handling data of Europeans. Also insightful was the fact that a lot of countries’ data privacy laws have now been updated using the comprehensive EU GDPR as a guideline.

    Later this week, I started learning HTML and how to use github in order to develop my e-portfolio for the M.SC program. I found this task quite simple and interesting. My e-portfolio design also got some positive feedback from my course mates.

    Introduction to Computer Architecture and the Functionality

    This week I learnt about the history of computers and how they have evolved over time. I attempted the preparation questions for seminar 1 titled “Introduction and Review of the Computer Architecture” which further solidified the learnings on functionalities of a computer.

    The class also continued the discussion on data protection. I read my course mates posts and made a few contributions. I also got a few comments on mine. I found this activity very valuable as it helped me to know my course mates and learn from them through the sharing of resources.

    Emerging trends in Computer Science

    Made another post on the EU GDPR, this time a summary of the initial post, and the subject of the last two weeks. It was mind boggling to discover the risks that emerging trends have brought to data protection especially with increased user profiles on social media platforms. On the positive side, these discoveries have instilled a new sense of discipline in me as a computing professional. Henceforth, i will pay more attention to data security and increase awareness in my workplace and amongst my friends and family.

    The formative activity for this week was to investigate some Google Artificial Intelligence tools and google tran- -slation technology.

    Computing Infrastructure and Network

    This week, I read about computer networks and communication protocols. I answered the preparation questions for the second seminar titled “Computing Infrastructure and Network” which took place this week.

    The formative activity for the week was to explore the functionalities of a web browser. I conducted my investi- -gation on Apple Safari and I did my first assignment on Codio, which is to create a simple website. Very easy!

    Software as a tool – Its functionality and Development Methodologies

    Our third seminar titled “Lab Exercises on Flode” took place this week.I completed most of the flode exercises on Codio which helped me learn the correct notations used in drawing flowcharts. I found some of the exercises on strings and loops a bit challenging and could not solve them correctly but in all I see how flowcharts make programming a lot easier and can help explain your code to a third party.

    Given that the OS is the most widely used software, the formative activity for the week was to pick an operating system of choice and then conduct some investigations on its functionality. As a mac user, I choose the MacOS
